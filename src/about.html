About Us

Welcome to HealthHive – a fun and engaging platform to turn your daily fitness tasks into exciting challenges. We believe that fitness should feel rewarding, motivating, and trackable like a game!

Whether you’re aiming to build consistency, push your limits, or simply stay active, our app helps you do it with daily goals, badges, and progress tracking.

This platform is proudly built by second-year B.Tech students who are passionate about health, technology, and meaningful design.
